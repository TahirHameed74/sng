Who we are

Abdul Hameed Gas Resources successfully put up its LPG Filling plant in the ideal location of Sharakpur during the year 2022. The machinery of the plant was installed to meet the requirement of the authority and as per international code NFPA-58. The plant can handle 60 M/Ton LPG in a single shift. We are supplying enough quantity of gas to our dealers to cater the need of Abdul Hameed Gas Resource Consumers either Industrial or Domestic. We procure the sizeable quantity of LPG from Local Producers & Imported Producer from Port Qasim, Karachi to fulfil the gap of demand and supply. We are committed to work under the policy Guideline of the Oil & Gas Regulatory Authority (OGRA) and ensure the Safety of the General Public.
